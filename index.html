Where to Go

Popular Destinations

Explore Odisha's most iconic landmarks and sacred sites

Sacred

Jagannath Temple, Puri

The Jagannath Temple is a sacred Hindu site in Puri, famous for its Rath Yatra festival and dedicated to Lord Jagannath.

Heritage

Lingaraj Temple, Bhubaneswar

One of the oldest temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its exquisite Kalinga architecture and cultural heritage.

Historic

Dhauli Giri, Bhubaneswar

A historic site where Emperor Ashoka embraced Buddhism, featuring the serene Shanti Stupa.
